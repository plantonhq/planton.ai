## Critical Success Factors

### 1. Start NOW
Don't wait for perfect. The deck is good enough. Start pitching this week.

### 2. Practice, Practice, Practice
Do at least 5 practice runs before your first real pitch.

### 3. Be Honest
Don't exaggerate numbers. Investors will find out. Honesty builds trust.

### 4. Show Passion
Your excitement is contagious. If you're not excited, why should they be?

### 5. Follow Up Fast
Send follow-up email within 24 hours of every meeting. Speed shows professionalism.

### 6. Keep Building
Don't stop shipping product while fundraising. Live traction is best traction.

### 7. Learn from Rejections
Every "no" teaches you something. Ask for feedback and improve.
