## Red Flags (Stop and Reassess If...)

### Fundraising Red Flags
üö© 50+ investor contacts, <5 meetings (deck or intro quality issue)
üö© 10+ pitches, zero term sheets (positioning or traction issue)
üö© Multiple investors say "come back when you have more traction" (too early)
üö© Multiple investors say "market too small" (positioning issue)
üö© 3+ months fundraising, no close (need to adjust valuation or terms)

### Product/Market Red Flags
üö© MRR not growing (product-market fit issue)
üö© High churn (product quality issue)
üö© No organic signups (marketing/positioning issue)
üö© Low usage (product value issue)

### Team/Execution Red Flags
üö© Can't hire after closing round (compensation or mission issue)
üö© Founder burnout (need to pace yourself!)
üö© Missing milestones badly (over-promised)
üö© Co-founder conflict (if you bring one on)

**If you see red flags, pause and address them. Don't just push through.**
